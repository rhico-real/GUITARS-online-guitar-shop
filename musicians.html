davie504

Davide Biale (born: April 5, 1994 [age 26]), better known online as Davie504, is an Italian YouTuber and musician born in Savona, Italy. He is known for uploading videos of his bass guitar.

Nando Miguel

Nando G. Miguel started playing the guitar before he had one in his hands. He started phrasing and composing before caressing its six strings for the very first time.

Sungha Jung

Sungha Jung is a real phenomenon! Who would have thought that a finger style guitarist, a string plucker, would one day need security guards to prevent him being mobbed by screaming teenage girls?

Ulli Bögershausen

Through his many concerts and workshops he has enthused and inspired generations, making them guitar crazy. For more than 30 years he has been at home on stages in Germany and internationally.

Steven Law

A pillar of Hong Kong’s guitar scene since 2008, Steven Law is one of East Asia’s best-known guitarists. His playing and arranging bring the rigour of classical training to modern acoustic fingerstyle guitar. At the age of 24 he became the first artist to represent Lakewood Guitars in Hong Kong.
